Something else?

Since I lived in China during my childhood,
I was obsessed with “QQ farm”-- a web game derived from a Chinese social software.
It's a interactive game. You can buy seeds to grow vegetables and sell them for money when you collect them, and then buy more advanced seeds.
Also, You can steal vegetables from your friends or help spray pesticides on them to help them grow!
If you don't want your vegetables stolen you can get a dog, but it's expensive and doesn't last long.
All in all, it was played by both adults and children at the time.
